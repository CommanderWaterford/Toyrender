Frequently Asked Questions

Can recruiters detect AI-generated headshots?

Recruiters can correctly identify AI-generated headshots only 39.5% of the time, according to the Ringover study. Despite 80% believing they can accurately spot AI headshots, they're actually incorrect over half the time (52.9%). Premium AI services are detected even less frequently‚Äîonly 29.2% of the time.

Do recruiters prefer AI headshots or real photos?

76.5% of recruiters preferred AI headshots when they didn't know which photos were AI-generated. Premium AI headshots ($35) were preferred by 42% of recruiters, while mid-range AI headshots ($9.99) were chosen by 34.4%, compared to only 23.5% preferring real headshots.

Should I disclose that my headshot is AI-generated?

88% of recruiters believe candidates should disclose AI-generated headshots. However, two-thirds (66%) said they would be put off by a candidate using an AI headshot if they recognized it. This creates a paradox‚Äîtransparency is valued, but AI usage may carry stigma. Focus on using high-quality AI that looks natural and professional rather than obviously artificial.

Are high-quality AI headshots harder to detect?

Yes. Recruiters identified free AI tools as fake 58.9% of the time, but top-tier AI services were detected only 29.2% of the time. Premium AI headshots ($35) are significantly harder to distinguish from professional photography, making them a better investment if you want natural-looking results.
