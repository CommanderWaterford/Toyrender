How AI Figurine Generation Works

AI figurine generation is a 2D creative process. You upload a selfie, choose a style or prompt, and Toyrender's AI engine (based on Google's Nano Banana model) imagines you as a figurine.

The Process:

Upload your selfie
Choose style & prompt
AI generates figurine image
Download in seconds

The result is fast, affordable, and doesn't require any equipment beyond your phone camera. Perfect for digital keepsakes and creative projects.

How Traditional 3D Scans Work

Traditional scanning captures actual geometry. Using structured light, laser scanners, or photogrammetry, a precise 3D mesh of your body or object is generated.

The Process:

Set up scanning equipment
Capture multiple angles
Process 3D mesh data
Clean and optimize model

This model can then be used for physical 3D printing, animation, or CAD work. The result is precise but requires professional equipment or studio visits.
