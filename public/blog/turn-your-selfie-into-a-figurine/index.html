What You'll Learn

Ever dreamed of seeing yourself as a collectible figurine? With Toyrender, it's possible. Using AI technology powered by Google's Nano Banana model, Toyrender transforms an everyday selfie into a lifelike figurine-style image in just seconds.

1

Snap a Perfect Selfie

The foundation of a great figurine image is a strong source photo. Here's what works best:

💡

Lighting

Use soft daylight or diffuse indoor lights. Avoid harsh shadows or backlight.

🎨

Background

Keep it simple. A neutral wall or uncluttered space helps the AI focus on you.

📸

Resolution

Aim for high-resolution photos (at least 1024px wide). Sharpness matters.

💡 Pro Tip

Toyrender accepts JPG or PNG up to 10MB. A recent smartphone photo is usually perfect!

2

Upload Your Photo

Visit Toyrender.com and log in. Upload your selfie via the "Choose Photo" button. Your original photo will appear on the left, ready for transformation.

Make sure your face is fully visible and well-lit. Once uploaded, you'll proceed to styling.
