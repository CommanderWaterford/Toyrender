Frequently Asked Questions

What types of AI photos can I create? ⌄
Our AI photo studio supports multiple categories: figurines (anime, space explorer, cyberpunk), professional business headshots, dating profile photos, and custom AI photo styles. Our Seedream 4 and Google Gemini AI models can render your photo in any theme you choose.
Do I own the generated AI photos? ⌄
Yes. With the Pro Pack you receive a commercial-use license for all your AI-generated outputs including figurines, dating photos, and business headshots. Free Trial results are for personal, non-commercial use.
How long does AI photo generation take? ⌄
Usually under a minute; during busy periods it can take a bit longer. You'll see a live status while we generate your AI photos using our advanced Seedream 4 and Google Gemini models.
What do I receive after AI photo generation? ⌄
A high-resolution AI-generated image in your chosen style - whether it's a figurine design, dating photo, business headshot, or other AI photo format - with a download option.
Are my photos private and secure? ⌄
Absolutely. We never publicly share your uploads. Photos are used to create your result in line with our Privacy Policy. Please avoid uploading sensitive images.
Do credits expire? ⌄
Credits are tied to your account and don't expire. If you run into any issue with a generation, contact support and we'll help fix it.
