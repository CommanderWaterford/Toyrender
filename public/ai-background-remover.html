Absolutely. The images you create are yours to use however you wish, including for commercial purposes like product listings, marketing campaigns, advertisements, book covers, and more.
How does the AI handle complex details like hair and fur?
Our tool is powered by advanced AI models from Google that are specifically trained to recognize and isolate fine details. It excels at handling challenging edges like hair, fur, and semi-transparent objects to provide a clean, professional-grade cutout.
What is the output resolution and format?
The output resolution matches your original image, up to our processing limits, ensuring high-quality results. The final download is always a PNG file, which is the standard format for preserving transparent backgrounds.
Are my uploaded photos safe and private?
Your privacy is our top priority. All uploaded images are processed securely and are automatically deleted from our servers within one hour. We do not use your images for AI training or share them with any third parties.
What kind of photos get the best results?
For optimal results, we recommend using images where the main subject is clear and well-defined. Photos with good lighting and a decent contrast between the subject and the background work best. However, our AI is powerful enough to handle busy and complex backgrounds too.
