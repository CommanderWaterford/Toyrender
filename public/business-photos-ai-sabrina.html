Business Photos: What makes them work

Professional photos balance clarity, expression, and context. Keep backgrounds simple, eyes crisp, and posture open. Choose color palettes that reflect your brand.

Quick checklist

  • Lead with a clean headshot (eye contact, soft light, neutral background).
  • Include one context shot (office, boardroom) and one personality shot (speaker or casual).
  • Keep attire tidy; avoid busy patterns; iron collars and lapels.
  • Export sRGB JPEG/WebP at 1200‚Äì1600px long edge for platforms like LinkedIn.
  • Write descriptive alt text (lighting, setting, expression, mood) for accessibility & SEO.
  • Add at least one banner‚Äëratio image (wide crop) for LinkedIn / conference pages.
