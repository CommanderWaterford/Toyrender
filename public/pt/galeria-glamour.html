Glamour AI FAQ

Quick answers about source photos, styling prompts, delivery timing, and licensing when you order the Toyrender Glamour Pack.

What kind of photo should I upload for the Glamour Pack?
Choose a sharp portrait with your full face visible, natural expression, and neutral lighting. Avoid sunglasses or heavy motion blur so the AI can capture hair, makeup, and facial details for the editorials you request.
Can I customize outfits, makeup, or lighting in the results?
Yes—each order lets you add styling cues like “silver sequin gown,” “soft glam makeup,” or “stage spotlight.” We mix these notes with the Glamour Pack prompt library to produce ten cohesive, high-fashion variations.
How fast will I get my AI glamour images?
Delivery usually completes in a few minutes. You’ll receive a link to review the full set right inside Toyrender, with instant downloads in high-resolution PNG or JPG formats.
Do I own the glamour portraits you generate for me?
Yes. Every Toyrender order includes a royalty-free commercial-use license. You can post the images on social media, press kits, album art, or marketing materials without additional fees.
Can I generate more than 10 glamour styled Photos?
Of course. The Gallery is just an example. You can generate as many Styles as you like, our pre defined Glamour Style Package generated 10 Photos.
