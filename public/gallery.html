About this Gallery — What Toyrender creates

This gallery showcases what Toyrender does best: turn a clear portrait photo into a figurine‑style image with premium lighting, a collectible look, and the signature round acrylic base. Each render is produced by our AI in seconds and delivered as a high‑resolution image that’s easy to save, print, or share.

You’ll see a range of aesthetics here — from cosplay (anime hero, ninja, mecha) and creative genres (cyberpunk, steampunk, fantasy) to real‑life moments (graduation, anniversaries) and practical use cases like business/LinkedIn avatars or gamer channel art. These examples are meant to inspire ideas you can try with your own photo.

Best photos to upload

  • Good, even lighting (soft indoor or daylight). Avoid strong backlight or deep shadows.
  • Face clearly visible, looking toward the camera; no sunglasses or heavy filters.
  • Simple background helps the AI outline you cleanly.
  • JPG/PNG up to 10 MB. Head‑and‑shoulders portraits work especially well.

From render to real life

Toyrender provides high‑resolution images (not STL files). Many customers print their favorite render for gifts, wall art, or merch; others share it on socials or hand it to a sculptor as a starting point for a physical model. If you’re planning a 3D print, bring the image to a professional who can model it in CAD or ZBrush and prepare it for printing.
