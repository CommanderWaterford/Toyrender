5 FAQ

Do you provide STL files for 3D printing? + –

No. Toyrender delivers high‑resolution images. For a physical figurine, share a render with a 3D artist who can model it for printing.

Are results good enough for LinkedIn? + –

Yes. Start with a well‑lit recent photo. See the Business Photos galleries for clean, brand‑safe looks suitable for corporate pages & resumes.

Can I generate several styles at once? + –

Yes. Upload once; batch prompts for studio, lifestyle, editorial, cosplay & glamour variations in a single run.

Who owns the outputs? + –

You do. Free results are personal use; Pro adds a commercial license. See the Privacy Policy.
