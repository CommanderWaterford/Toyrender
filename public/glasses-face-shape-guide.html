🔴Round Face

Characteristics:

  • • Equal width and length
  • • Soft curves and full cheeks
  • • Rounded chin

✓ Best Frames:

  • • Rectangle frames: Add definition
  • • Square frames: Create angles
  • • Geometric frames: Sharp contrast

✗ Avoid:

Round or oval frames that emphasize roundness

🥚Oval Face

Characteristics:

  • • Balanced proportions
  • • Forehead slightly wider than chin
  • • Gentle curves

✓ Best Frames:

  • • Almost anything! Most versatile
  • • Aviator: Classic choice
  • • Wayfarer: Timeless style
  • • Cat-eye: Playful sophistication

💡 Pro Tip:

Choose frames as wide as (or slightly wider than) the broadest part of your face

⬜Square Face

Characteristics:

  • • Strong jawline
  • • Broad forehead
  • • Angular features

✓ Best Frames:

  • • Round frames: Soften angles
  • • Oval frames: Classic contrast
  • • Cat-eye frames: Draw eyes up

✗ Avoid:

Heavy, boxy frames that emphasize angularity

💜Heart Face

Characteristics:

  • • Wider forehead
  • • High cheekbones
  • • Narrow, pointed chin

✓ Best Frames:

  • • Aviator: Wider at bottom
  • • Round frames: Soften chin
  • • Rimless: Minimize heaviness
  • • Light colors: Less attention to forehead

✗ Avoid:

Heavy top bars or decorative embellishments on top

💎Diamond Face

Characteristics:

  • • Narrow forehead and chin
  • • Wide cheekbones
  • • Angular features

✓ Best Frames:

  • • Cat-eye: Accentuate cheekbones
  • • Oval frames: Soften angles
  • • Rimless: Show bone structure
  • • Detailed frames: Draw to brow line

✗ Avoid:

Narrow frames that make cheekbones appear wider
