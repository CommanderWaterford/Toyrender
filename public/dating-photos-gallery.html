Quick Guide for Swipe-Worthy Photos

  • Lead with a clear solo portrait: eyes visible, natural smile, tidy background.
  • Use good light: window light or golden hour; avoid harsh overheads and mirror flash.
  • Show context: one hobby/pet/travel shot that sparks a message.
  • Keep it PG-13 and authentic; smart-casual outfits in solid colors work great.
  • Order idea: opener → context/hobby → full-body → one social → conversation-starter.
